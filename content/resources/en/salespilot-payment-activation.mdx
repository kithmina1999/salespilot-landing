# How SalesPilot payment and activation works

SalesPilot's current subscription path is designed for Sri Lankan businesses that prefer bank transfers. You choose a plan, transfer the amount, upload a screenshot, and receive an activation code after review.

## Current primary payment path

Bank transfer proof is the primary flow for now. This keeps payment simple for local businesses and gives the SalesPilot team a clear verification trail.

## 1. Pick a plan

From the dashboard, open Usage & Plan, then Subscribe. Choose Starter or Pro and select monthly or annual billing.

Current public prices are:

- Starter monthly: Rs. 1,490
- Starter annual: Rs. 14,900
- Pro monthly: Rs. 3,490
- Pro annual: Rs. 34,900

## 2. Transfer the exact amount

Transfer the amount shown in the dashboard to the displayed bank account. Use your registered WhatsApp number as the payment reference when possible.

Using the right reference helps the review team match your payment proof to the correct business account.

## 3. Upload the payment screenshot

Upload a clear JPG or PNG screenshot of the bank receipt. The app accepts image uploads and sends the proof for admin review.

If the screenshot is unclear, the proof may be rejected and you will need to resubmit it.

## 4. Wait for verification

After submission, your payment status becomes pending. The SalesPilot team checks the amount, reference, screenshot, and selected plan.

When approved, SalesPilot generates an activation code and sends it to your registered email.

## 5. Activate the plan

Open the Activate Code tab in SalesPilot and enter the activation code. Your plan updates after successful activation.

Monthly plans activate for 30 days. Annual plans activate for 365 days.

## Refund and policy notes

Refund rules are published in the SalesPilot Refund Policy. Online payments and card refunds may depend on PayHere rules when PayHere is used.

For payment-provider requirements, public policy pages should stay visible in the footer: Refund Policy, Privacy Policy, and Terms & Conditions.
